Hey, my name is Michail Zarečenskij. I’ve been working at JetBrains for about ten years, contributing to the Kotlin compiler, the IntelliJ IDEA plugin, and the broader Kotlin ecosystem. These days, my main focus is the Kotlin language.

I’ve started this blog as a space for more informal posts: thoughts, ideas that I occasionally just want to put into words and share. Many of them will, of course, relate to my work, though not necessarily represent it in full. So, take anything I say about the future with a grain of salt. And welcome! :)
